Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 June 2026

This policy applies to all of our iOS apps, including HEIC to JPG Converter and Blur Faces & License Plates, and any future apps we publish under the same developer account.

Short version: our apps do not collect, store, or transmit any of your data. Everything happens on your device.

What we collect

Nothing. Our apps have no accounts, no analytics, no advertising SDKs, and no servers. We never see your photos, files, or any information about you.

Your photos and files

Anything you open in our apps is read and processed entirely on your iPhone or iPad using Apple's on-device frameworks. Nothing you import is ever uploaded anywhere. Results are written to a temporary location on your device and are only shared when you explicitly choose to save or share them.

Permissions

An app may ask permission to add a result to your photo library only when you choose to save it. You select individual items to work on through Apple's system picker; the apps do not request access to your full library.

Purchases

One-time unlocks are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive your payment details. Apple's privacy policy governs that transaction.

Children

Our apps collect no data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page.
